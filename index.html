How to Generate a Premium Mock Test

  1. Copy the Prompt: Click the Copy AI Prompt button on the main page.
  2. Go to an AI: Paste this prompt into any advanced AI model (like ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini).
  3. Upload your Sources: Give the AI your study materials (book PDFs, images, links to previous year questions, notes, etc.). Provide as much content as you have!
  4. Generate & Copy: The AI will process everything and generate a complete Mock Test JSON code block. Copy this raw text.
  5. Initialize Test: Paste the JSON back into the text box on this website and click Validate & Initialize Test.
  6. Start & Focus: Enter your real name, read the instructions, and start.
    ⚠ IMPORTANT DISCIPLINE RULE: Once you start, you cannot leave the fullscreen or switch tabs for 3 hours. Doing so will instantly terminate and fail the test!
  7. Analyze: After the test, download the premium PDF report card to analyze your speed, stamina, weaknesses, and guessing habits.
